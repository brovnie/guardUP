Are you afraid of the dark?

Walking alone can be dangerous especially at night. How many times have you felt that someone was following you? Did you feel unsafe? Women are considered to be an easy target but it doesn't have to be that way.

What is Guard up

Guard up is a mobile application and smart ring invented for women who wants to feel safe when they walk alone. With Guard-up ring you don't need to wory about no internet connection. Share your location with your friends and family. Alert to your friends or directly call the police at the press of a button.

How does it work?

Your guardians

Create a list of 6 guardians who you can reach in case of danger.

Share location

Share your location with your friends and family. Choose a destination, set up the timer and safety area. If you don't reach your destination your guardians will be informed.

Keep an eye on each other

With party mode you can share your location with other members in the party and track each others position. Set up a safety area and get notified when one of you is out of range.

Call police

The Guard up application changes the police number based on your location.

Ring specifications

Guard up ring works perfectly without a smartphone. Guard up ring has a built in sim card and gps.

Press the button for 2 seconds to alarm your friends about danger or 5 seconds to notify the police.

You are able to record a short video and audio.
